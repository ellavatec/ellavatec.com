EllaVatec Helping companies reshape their culture and diversify their workforce

EllaVatec, working to help employers discover and correct the pain points of finding and hiring a more diverse workforce

Based in Ann Arbor, MI the the founder and lead consultant at EllaVatec has over twenty years of experience in technology.

Often finding herself as a the only female developer at her workplace she is now seeking to solve this discrepancy by using her personal experience to help companies create a more inclusive and diverse workforce.
